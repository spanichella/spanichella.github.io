Research Ecosystem & Collaborations

My research activities are built around a broad international ecosystem of industrial partners, universities, research laboratories, and innovation stakeholders. These collaborations support joint research projects, scientific publications, technology transfer activities, competitive research proposals, and the supervision of students and junior researchers.

Over the years, I have established collaborations across Europe, North America, Asia, and Australia, addressing interdisciplinary challenges at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, Autonomous and Cyber-Physical Systems, Robotics, Safety-Critical Systems, DevOps, and AI-enabled Industry 4.0 applications.
